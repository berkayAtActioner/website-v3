About Actioner

Revenue predictability, built on evidence.

Actioner is the AI execution layer for revenue teams. We pull context from emails, meetings, and your systems, then turn it into deal and account intelligence you can act on — with receipts.

Who Are We?

Customer-success, support, account and product teams all own a piece of the retention puzzle but the signals they need live in different tools. Actioner brings those signals together, turning scattered data into AI agents that spot risk, surface growth opportunities and keep every team on the same page.

What we believe

AI should remove admin, not add process.

Great teams should be talking to customers, not updating fields.

Context wins.

If AI can't see the whole deal, it can't help you run it.

Trust needs receipts.

Every insight should point back to the source.
